# Rand Medical Center — Blog Topical Authority Plan

**Goal:** Establish RMC as the authoritative voice for work injury, auto accident, pain management, and orthopedic care in the northwest Chicago suburbs. Capture local + national long-tail SEO traffic and feed both `/personal-injury` and `/work-injury` hub pages.

**Existing inventory:** 36 articles already published in `rand-medical-content/src/content/articles/`. The plan below maps existing articles to their cluster, then adds new pillar + supporting articles to fill gaps.

**Internal linking rule:**
- Every supporting post links UP to its pillar article (in body and via "Read the full guide" CTA).
- Every supporting post links LATERALLY to 2–3 related supports.
- Every pillar links DOWN to all its supports + UP to the relevant hub page (`/work-injury` or `/personal-injury`) + relevant service pillar (`/services/pain-management`, etc.).

## Internal Linking Map (Pillar → Supports)

For each cluster, after writing the pillar:

```
PILLAR ARTICLE (2,000+ words)
    ├── Links DOWN to: every support article in the cluster
    ├── Links UP to: relevant hub page (/personal-injury or /work-injury)
    ├── Links LATERAL to: 1–2 other pillar articles (only when topically relevant)
    └── Links to relevant SERVICE PAGES (e.g., /services/pain-management/epidural-steroid-injections)

SUPPORT ARTICLE (800–1,200 words)
    ├── Links UP to: cluster pillar (with "Read the full guide" CTA at the end)
    ├── Links LATERAL to: 2–3 other supports in the same cluster
    ├── Links to: 1 relevant service page or condition page
    └── Optional: Link to 1 other cluster's pillar if topically connected
```

## Publishing Cadence (Suggested)

- **Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4):** Write & publish 5 pillar articles (one per cluster). High word count, deep content.
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 5–10):** Publish remaining 17 new support articles at 3–4 per week.
- **Phase 3 (Ongoing):** Audit existing articles. Update internal links to point at the new pillars + hub pages.

After publication, add `relatedArticles` arrays to each article's frontmatter so MDX renderer can pull the related-reading row automatically.
